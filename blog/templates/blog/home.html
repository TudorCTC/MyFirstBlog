Welcome!

Here you will find my reviews of video games that I have played, reviews of books that I have read and posts about my experience as a Computer Science student!

So what types of books and games can you expect to see in my reviews? Well...

My favourite genre of video games is represented by RPGs, but I also enjoyed quite a few FPS games, both singleplayer and multiplayer, as well as some great platformers and some games that really do not fit in a specific genre.

As for books, I mostly read fiction books, as I find immersion to be a really important aspect of the content I consumme and for me, non-fiction books are too "detached" to really keep me interested. While I usually choose books by how interesting they sound, not by genre, I can definitely say that historical fiction, fantasy sagas and psychological novels end up on my bookshelf the most.

With this being said, I always try to be open-minded and experience new things, so I will keep you on your toes!

Enjoy! :)
